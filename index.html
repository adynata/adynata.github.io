ARK OF THE MOON

  • ABOUT
  • CREDITS
Envisioned, designed, and fabricated over the course of a few weeks during the summer of 2014, The Ark of the Moon is a 16' inflatable floating structure meant to recall the moon rising over water. The moon is made from two nested geodesic domes crafted from ripstop nylon, bushels of seam tape, a surfeit of thread, one tiny blower fan, the care, skill, and craftsmanship of many dear friends/fans of inflatable floating architechture, and was designed and engineered in collaboration with Mike Estee.

Since its first flight at Camp Tipsy, it has also appeared at the premier Figment Oakland, a festival celebrating interactive art, and as part of an immersive theatrical work exploring Heironomous Bosch's Garden of Earthly delights, where it served as the backdrop for Fallen Cosmos University.
