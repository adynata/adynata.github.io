ARK OF THE MOON

  • ABOUT
  • CREDITS
Envisioned, designed, and fabricated over the course of a few weeks during the summer of 2014, The Ark of the Moon is a 16' inflatable floating structure meant to recall the moon rising over water. The moon is made from two nested geodesic domes crafted from ripstop nylon, bushels of seam tape, a surfeit of thread, one tiny blower fan, the care, skill, and craftsmanship of many dear friends and fans of inflatable floating architechture, and was designed and engineered in collaboration with Mike Estee.
